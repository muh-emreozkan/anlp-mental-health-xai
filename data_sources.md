# Dataset Sources

## 1. DEPTWEET
- **Source:** Twitter mental health dataset
- **Original paper:** DEPTWEET: A Typology for Social Media Texts to Detect Depression Severities
- **Access:** Rehydration required (tweet IDs only)
- **Size:** 5,474 tweets (after augmentation)
- **Classes:** mild, moderate, severe, non-depressed

## 2. Reddit Suicide Detection
- **Source:** Kaggle - Suicide and Depression Detection
- **Link:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nikhileswarkomati/suicide-watch
- **Size:** 232,074 posts
- **Classes:** suicide, non-suicide

## 3. C-SSRS Clinical Validation
- **Source:** Research repository
- **Access:** Contact original authors
- **Link** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/thedevastator/c-ssrs-labeled-suicidality-in-500-anonymized-red
- **Size:** 500 psychologist-rated posts
- **Classes:** Ideation, Behavior, Attempt, Supportive, Indicator
